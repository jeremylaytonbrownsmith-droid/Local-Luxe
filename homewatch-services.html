Home Watch Services

Routine professional inspections that keep your property safe, secure, and worry-free while you're away.

what we do

Your Property, Protected

When you're away from your home — whether for a season or a few weeks — a lot can go wrong unnoticed. A slow leak, a failed HVAC system, or storm damage can quickly escalate into costly repairs. That's where Local Luxe Concierge comes in.

Our Home Watch service provides scheduled, professional visual inspections of your property. We look for anything out of the ordinary, document our findings with photos, and send you a detailed report after every visit — so you always know the condition of your home.

We serve homeowners in Bluffton, Hilton Head, Beaufort, and Okatie, SC with a personal touch that larger property management companies simply can't offer. Your second home gets the same level of care and attention as your primary residence.

"Home Watch is a visual inspection of a home or property, looking for obvious issues."

— National Home Watch Association

trusted access control

Key-Holder Services

As your on-the-ground key holder, we coordinate access for emergencies, vendors, and guests — so your home is always in the right hands.

Emergency Access

We respond quickly to alarm activations, water leaks, HVAC failures, storm damage, and lockouts — coordinating the right professionals and keeping you informed every step of the way.

Vendor & Contractor Entry

We meet contractors and service providers, provide access, and oversee work while you're away — so you never need to hand out copies of your key or coordinate from afar.

Guest & Family Access

We coordinate arrivals and departures, provide home access and walk-throughs, and make sure your home is ready and welcoming for every guest or family member.
