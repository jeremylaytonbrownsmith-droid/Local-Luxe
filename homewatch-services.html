Your Property, Protected

When you're away from your home — whether for a season or a few weeks — a lot can go wrong unnoticed. A slow leak, a failed HVAC system, or storm damage can quickly escalate into costly repairs. That's where Local Luxe Concierge comes in.

Our Home Watch service provides scheduled, professional visual inspections of your property. We look for anything out of the ordinary, document our findings with photos, and send you a detailed report after every visit — so you always know the condition of your home.

We serve homeowners in Bluffton, Hilton Head, Beaufort, and Okatie, SC with a personal touch that larger property management companies simply can't offer. Your second home gets the same level of care and attention as your primary residence.

What is Home Watch?

"Home Watch is a visual inspection of a home or property, looking for obvious issues."

–National Home Watch Association
every visit

What We Inspect

Each Home Watch visit covers a thorough visual inspection of your property, inside and out. You receive a written report with photos after every visit.

Interior Walkthrough

Full interior inspection checking for water intrusion, unusual odors, signs of pests, and general condition of living areas.

HVAC & Systems

Checking heating, cooling, and ventilation systems are operating normally; thermostat settings verified.

Plumbing & Water

Inspecting for leaks under sinks, around water heaters, toilets, and visible pipes. Water pressure checks.

Appliances

Visual check of major appliances including refrigerator, stove, washer/dryer, and dishwasher for proper operation.

Windows & Doors

Checking locks, seals, and frames. Looking for any signs of break-in, water infiltration, or damage.

Security Check

Alarm systems, motion lights, and exterior entry points inspected. Mailbox and package overflow addressed.

Exterior & Roof

Checking roof, gutters, siding, and foundation for visible damage, debris, or storm-related issues.

Storm Preparation

Pre-storm securing of outdoor furniture and property. Post-storm inspection and damage documentation.

Detailed Report

After every visit you receive a written report with timestamped photos documenting the condition of your property.
