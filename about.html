About Local Luxe Concierge

A Lowcountry-based service built on trust, personal connection, and an unwavering commitment to your peace of mind.

who we are

Built on Trust,
Driven by Care

Local Luxe Concierge was founded with a simple belief: homeowners deserve thoughtful, professional support that goes beyond what any large management company can offer. We combine deep roots in the Lowcountry community with a unique background in both property management and healthcare — giving us an exceptionally thorough, detail-oriented approach to every service we provide.

We specialize in caring for second homes and non-rental properties in Bluffton, Hilton Head, Beaufort, and Okatie — the places where our clients come to rest, reconnect, and recharge. When you're not here, we treat your home as if it were our own.

Every client relationship begins with a conversation. We take the time to understand your home, your preferences, and what matters most to you. That personal connection is at the heart of everything we do.

To provide personalized, professional assistance that brings comfort, convenience, and care to your everyday life.
