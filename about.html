why we serve

Our Mission

Local Luxe Concierge believes in being present for the most important moments in life. When we are focused on being present, we can experience life more fully and appreciate what matters most.

Chances are that your second home is your getaway — an opportunity to push pause. What if you stayed a little longer, not because of a to-do list, but because the stress of home management was replaced with more meaningful time?

Danny Thornberry — Founder & CEO

Danny Thornberry, Founder and CEO of Local Luxe Concierge, brings a unique blend of real estate expertise, property management experience, and community leadership to the business he co-founded with his wife.

uniquely qualified

Built for This

With a combination of property management and medical experience, we have built relationships in Bluffton and Hilton Head for over 5 years. The businesses and specialists we hire are chosen for their professionalism, service, and sense of detail.

Dr. Lauren Thornberry — Co-Owner

Dr. Lauren Thornberry brings a family-oriented and compassionate perspective to Local Luxe Concierge, shaped by years of experience caring for patients as a dedicated physician. Her medical background gives the team an exceptionally thorough, detail-oriented approach to every service they provide.

what we do

How We Serve You

The Local Luxe Concierge team provides personalized concierge services and full home management, focused on delivering on our promise to each individual client.

We serve clients who are using their home for themselves and their family — not as income property or rental. We work to understand our clients' lifestyle and anticipate needs, proactively managing tasks that would otherwise prevent our clients from enjoying their time at home.

One of the biggest values to our clients is our list of reputable vendors. Our high-performing team can oversee projects large and small. From tree removal to commissioning an artist to enhance a space, we're often the one-stop resource for most home-related needs.
