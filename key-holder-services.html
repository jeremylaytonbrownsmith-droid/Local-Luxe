Key-Holder Services

Secure, documented access management — so the right people get in, and you always know who did.

what we provide

Your Trusted Key Holder

When you're away from your Lowcountry home, the last thing you want is to worry about who has access to it. Local Luxe Concierge serves as your trusted, on-the-ground key holder — available to coordinate entry for emergencies, scheduled services, and pre-approved vendors whenever you need us.

We maintain a secure, documented record of every key we hold and every time your property is accessed. Whether it's an unexpected plumbing emergency or a scheduled appliance delivery, we're the reliable local contact that gives you complete confidence your home is in good hands.

Our key-holder service pairs seamlessly with our Home Watch and Concierge offerings — giving you a single, trusted point of contact for all things related to your property's access and care.

our services

What's Included

Every key-holder arrangement is handled with care, discretion, and a full chain-of-custody record for your peace of mind.

Emergency Access

We hold a secure key to your property so that emergency contractors or utility professionals can gain access immediately when you can't be there.

Trusted Entry Coordination

We greet and let in pre-approved vendors, inspectors, and service professionals on your behalf — so you never have to hand out copies of your key.

Secure Key Management

Your key is stored in a secure, documented system with a full chain-of-custody record. You always know exactly where your key is and when it was used.

Peace of Mind

Never wonder who has access to your home. Every entry is logged, reported back to you promptly, and documented with notes on what took place.
