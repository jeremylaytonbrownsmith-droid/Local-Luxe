Key-Holder Services
by Local Luxe Concierge

We securely maintain access to your home and provide reliable, discreet support when you can't be there — so your home is always in good hands.

what we provide

Your Trusted Key Holder

When you're away from your Lowcountry home, the last thing you want is to worry about who has access to it. Local Luxe Concierge serves as your trusted, on-the-ground key holder — available to coordinate entry for emergencies, scheduled services, and pre-approved vendors whenever you need us.

We maintain a secure, documented record of every key we hold and every time your property is accessed. Whether it's an unexpected plumbing emergency or a scheduled appliance delivery, we're the reliable local contact that gives you complete confidence your home is in good hands.

Our key-holder service pairs seamlessly with our Home Watch and Concierge offerings — giving you a single, trusted point of contact for all things related to your property's access and care.

how we help

Key-Holder Services Tailored to Your Lifestyle

From emergency access to routine home checks, we're here to help protect your home and give you peace of mind — wherever life takes you.

Emergency Access Coordination

We respond quickly to provide access for emergencies such as alarm activations, water leaks, HVAC failures, storm damage, and lockouts.

Vendor & Contractor Access

We'll meet contractors or service providers, provide access, and ensure work is completed while you're away — so you never need to hand out copies of your key.

Guest & Family Access

We coordinate arrivals and departures, provide home access and walk-throughs, and make sure your home is ready and welcoming for every guest.

Second-Home Support

Scheduled home checks, mail and package retrieval, storm preparation, pre-arrival home prep, and post-departure access checks — all handled for you.

Mail & Package Management

We collect your mail and packages regularly to keep your home secure, prevent unwanted attention, and forward anything important when needed.

Home Monitoring & Peace of Mind

We keep an eye on the details so you don't have to. Every entry is logged and reported back to you — so your home is always in good hands.
