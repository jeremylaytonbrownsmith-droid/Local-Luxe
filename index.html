about us

Our Mission

Local Luxe Concierge offers Personalized Concierge Services and non-rental home management with a focus on Home Watch and Concierge Services for homeowners in Bluffton, Hilton Head and Beaufort, South Carolina.

For those who desire more, our tailored Personalized Concierge Services provide an elevated level of care—from running errands to digital/tech help to scheduling appointments to transportation to appointments.

Taking care of second homes is our specialty. Home Concierge is the resource for homeowners who aren't renting their home to vacation travelers.

Our Home Watch services provide routine visits for away homeowners, while our add-on Concierge options deliver elevated care and upkeep—ensuring complete peace of mind.

To provide personalized, professional assistance that brings comfort, convenience, and care to your everyday life.
