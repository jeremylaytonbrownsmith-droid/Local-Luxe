our services

How We Can Help

We offer a wide range of personalized services tailored to your lifestyle. If you don't see what you need below, just ask — we're here to help.

Errand Running

Grocery pickup, dry cleaning, prescription retrieval, post office runs, and any other errands that take time out of your day.

Transportation

Coordination of transportation to medical appointments, airports, special events, and day trips throughout the Lowcountry.

Tech & Digital Help

Setting up smart home devices, phones, tablets, and streaming services. Troubleshooting connectivity and app assistance.

Appointment Scheduling

Booking and managing medical, salon, service, and lifestyle appointments. We coordinate the calendar so you don't have to.

Pre-Arrival Prep

Before you arrive, we'll stock your fridge, turn on utilities, adjust the thermostat, and have your home ready to welcome you.

Vendor Coordination

We schedule, meet, and oversee contractors, repair technicians, cleaners, and service providers on your behalf.
