who we serve

Your Time Is Valuable. Let Us Handle the Rest.

Local Luxe clients share a common wish — to be fully present for the moments that matter most. They're ready to hand off the responsibilities of maintenance, errands, and readiness to a trusted partner they can count on from anywhere.

add-on services

How We Can Help

Each service below can be added to your existing Home Watch plan or arranged as a standalone. If you don't see what you need, just ask.

Subcontractor Coordination

We schedule, meet, and oversee contractors and repair technicians on your behalf — including construction projects of any size — so you never need to coordinate from afar.

Seasonal Preparation

We prepare your home for every season — storm readiness, winterization, holiday prep, and pre-arrival setup so your home is waiting exactly the way you like it.

Ongoing Maintenance

Coordination of routine maintenance including HVAC filter changes, pest control, lawn care, pool service, and housekeeping oversight — everything running, always.

Emergency Liaison

When urgent issues arise, we respond immediately — coordinating the right professionals and keeping you fully informed every step of the way, no matter where you are.

Errands & Personal Assistance

Grocery pickup, prescription retrieval, dry cleaning, and other personal tasks handled locally so your home and life run smoothly from a distance.

Experience Management

Restaurant reservations, event tickets, tee times, activity planning, and curated experiences — so every visit to the Lowcountry is exactly what you want it to be.
