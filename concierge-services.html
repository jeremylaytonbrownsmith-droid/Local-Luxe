what we offer

Elevate Your Home Watch Plan

Our Home Watch plan keeps your property safe while you're away — but sometimes you need more than inspections. Our Concierge add-on services fill in all the gaps, giving you a fully hands-off home management experience from wherever you are in the world.

From coordinating contractors to preparing your home for a new season, our concierge team handles the details that require a trusted, reliable presence on the ground. Every service is performed by someone who knows your property and treats it with the same care they'd give their own.

Concierge services can be bundled with any Home Watch plan or arranged individually as needed. Just let us know what you need — we're here to make home ownership simpler and more enjoyable.

add-on services

How We Can Help

Each service below can be added to your existing Home Watch plan or arranged as a standalone. If you don't see what you need, just ask.

Subcontractor Coordination

We schedule, meet, and oversee contractors and repair technicians on your behalf so you never need to coordinate from afar.

Seasonal Preparation

We prepare your home for seasonal changes — including storm season readiness, winterization, and pre-arrival setup before you return.

Ongoing Maintenance

Coordination of routine maintenance services including HVAC filter changes, pest control treatments, lawn care, and pool service oversight.

Emergency Liaison

When urgent issues arise at your property, we respond immediately — coordinating the right professionals and keeping you fully informed every step of the way.

Personal Assistance

Errands, grocery pickup, prescription retrieval, and other personal tasks handled locally so your home and life run smoothly from a distance.

Experience Management

Restaurant reservations, event tickets, activity planning, and curated experiences for when you're in town and want to make the most of the Lowcountry.
