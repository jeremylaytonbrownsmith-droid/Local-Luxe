the process

How We Work Together

Contact Us

Contact us by phone or by completing the form and a member of our team will be pleased to provide additional information about our services, learn more about your specific needs, and determine the best service approach for your property. We will also schedule a complimentary consultation, as every home and client requires a customized level of care and attention.

Initial Consultation

Danny Thornberry, Owner, will personally schedule a time to discuss your needs, review your schedule and service preferences, provide pricing information, and answer any questions you may have. This consultation may be conducted by phone or during a private meeting at your home for a more personalized experience.

Review Your Custom Plan

Once we have a thorough understanding of what you are looking for — whether Lifestyle Concierge Services, Home Watch Services, or a customized combination of both — we will develop a tailored program designed specifically for your lifestyle, property and personal preferences.

Sit Back and Relax

Our team will keep you fully informed following each visit to your home via our proprietary software. If you have any questions or concerns, your dedicated Administrative Concierge is just a call, text, or email away! We will always reach out directly if there is anything in the home that requires your immediate attention.
