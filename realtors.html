Home Watch Services for Realtors

Partner with Local Luxe Concierge to give your buyers peace of mind after closing. Our professional Home Watch and concierge services help monitor and care for vacant and seasonal homes.

Partner With Us Today ⟶

Add Value for Your Clients

At Local Luxe Concierge, we partner with real estate professionals to help provide their clients with an extra layer of care, security, and peace of mind after the closing.

Many buyers in the Lowcountry purchase second homes, vacation properties, or investment properties that may sit vacant for extended periods of time. A professional Home Watch service ensures those homes are regularly checked, maintained, and protected.

By referring your clients to Local Luxe Concierge, you're giving them a trusted resource that helps safeguard their new investment long after the transaction is complete.

How We Support Your Clients

We assist with move-in coordination by meeting with movers, contractors, appliance deliveries, cable and internet installers, painters, and cleaning crews. Our team can recommend trusted local vendors, oversee scheduled work, and perform simple handyman tasks such as hanging curtains to help ensure the home does not appear vacant.

Property Protection

  • Scheduled Home Watch inspections
  • Detailed reports with photos
  • Early detection of maintenance issues

Property Management Support

  • Vendor and contractor coordination
  • Ongoing maintenance oversight
  • Storm preparation and response

Client Convenience

  • Pre-arrival home preparation
  • Move-in and move-out services
  • Concierge services tailored to their needs

Why Realtors Partner With Us

Our goal is simple — help your clients protect their investment while making you look great for connecting them with a reliable local resource.

Working with Local Luxe Concierge helps you:

  • Provide added value and peace of mind to your buyers
  • Build long-term relationships with your clients
  • Ensure vacant homes are properly monitored and maintained
  • Have a trusted local partner when clients are out of town
Ideal Clients for Home Watch

Your clients benefit most from our services if they:

  • Own a second home
  • Have a vacation property
  • Live out of state
  • Rent their home seasonally
  • Are not occupying the property full-time
