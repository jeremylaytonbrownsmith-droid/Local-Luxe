Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Home Watch and Lifestyle Concierge services.

What is Home Watch?

Home Watch, as defined by the National Home Watch Association, is a visual inspection of a home or property, looking for obvious issues.

What's the difference between Home Watch services and Concierge Services?

Home Watch offers a routine checklist with detailed GPS-enabled reporting. Most requests outside of the routine checklist would be considered Concierge Services.

How do I become a client?
  • Schedule a complimentary 30-minute consultation in person, by phone, or through video call. Submit an inquiry or call 843.338.2851.
  • We'll work to understand your needs and recommend the best way to work together — whether hourly, per visit, a package, or a membership.
  • You'll receive a client agreement which must be signed and returned before the start of your services.
Are you insured and licensed?

Yes! In order to protect ourselves and our clients we are licensed, insured, and bonded.

How often will my home be inspected?

Local Luxe offers flexible inspection plans including weekly, bi-monthly, and monthly visits. For most homes, we recommend at least two visits per month to help ensure your property remains safe and well maintained while you are away. Customized service plans can also be created to meet the specific needs of each property.

How can I be confident my home is being properly cared for while I'm away?

Every visit is documented through our reporting system with time-stamped, dated, and GPS-verified photos. You'll receive a detailed inspection report after each visit so you always know the condition of your home.

How do you communicate with clients?

We believe communication should be simple and prompt. After each visit, you will receive a detailed inspection report by email within 24 hours. If an urgent issue is discovered, we will notify you immediately by phone or text.

Why do I need to sign an agreement?

An agreement communicates your expectations for Local Luxe Concierge. It also protects you and us. If something happens to one of our employees while we are at your home, or items of yours are damaged, destroyed, or missing, an insurance claim can be filed with our provider. Without an agreement we are unable to provide you with services.

Which areas do you serve?
  • We serve Beaufort County and Jasper County in South Carolina. Service areas include but are not limited to Bluffton, Hilton Head, Beaufort, Okatie, Hardeeville, Ridgeland, Fripp Island, Harbor Island, and St. Helena Island.
  • Locations outside of a 25-mile radius of 29910 may be considered and will be subject to standard mileage fees.
