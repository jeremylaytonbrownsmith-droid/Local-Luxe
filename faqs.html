Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Home Watch and Lifestyle Concierge services.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve homeowners throughout the South Carolina Lowcountry, including Bluffton, Hilton Head Island, Beaufort, and Okatie. If you're unsure whether we cover your area, please reach out — we're happy to discuss your specific location.

What is the difference between Home Watch and property management?

Home Watch is specifically designed for non-rental properties — second homes, vacation homes, and seasonal residences that are not rented out to guests. A property manager typically handles rental logistics, tenant relations, and lease agreements.

Our Home Watch service focuses on keeping your unoccupied home safe, maintained, and properly monitored through routine visual inspections, detailed reports, and vendor coordination — without the rental management overhead.

How often will you visit my home?

We offer three visit frequency options: Monthly, Bi-Weekly (twice per month), and Weekly. The right frequency depends on factors like how long you'll be away, the age and systems in your home, and your personal comfort level.

We're happy to discuss which plan is the best fit during your free initial consultation.

What does a Home Watch visit include?

Each visit is a thorough visual inspection of your property, both interior and exterior. We check HVAC systems, plumbing, appliances, windows and doors, signs of pests or water intrusion, security, exterior condition, and more.

After every visit you receive a written report with timestamped photos documenting the condition of your home, along with any notes or recommendations.

What happens if you find a problem during a visit?

We contact you immediately if we find anything that requires your attention. Depending on the urgency and your preferences, we can coordinate with vendors and contractors on your behalf, or simply document and report the issue for you to handle directly.

We work to be your single point of contact — keeping you informed without overwhelming you.

Are you insured and bonded?

Yes. Local Luxe Concierge is fully insured and bonded. We can provide proof of insurance upon request. Your home and your trust are our most important responsibilities.

How do I receive visit reports?

After each visit you'll receive a detailed written report by email, including timestamped photos and notes from the inspection. For urgent findings, we'll also contact you by phone or text immediately.

We believe in full transparency — you should always know exactly what's happening at your home, even from thousands of miles away.

Can I add Lifestyle Concierge services to my Home Watch plan?

Absolutely. Our Home Watch and Lifestyle Concierge services are designed to complement each other. You can add concierge services — such as pre-arrival preparation, errand running, vendor coordination, or tech assistance — to any Home Watch plan at any time.

Many of our clients find that combining both services gives them complete peace of mind and a seamless experience when they return to their Lowcountry home.

What if I only need services occasionally, not on a regular schedule?

We understand that every homeowner's needs are different. While our Home Watch plans are scheduled services, our Lifestyle Concierge offerings can be arranged on an as-needed basis. Contact us to discuss a plan that fits your schedule and situation.

How do I get started?

Getting started is easy. Simply reach out to us through our contact form, by email at localluxeconcierge@gmail.com, or by phone at 843.338.2851.

We'll schedule a free, no-obligation consultation to learn about your home, discuss your needs, and recommend the right service plan for you.

Do you provide services for rental properties?

Our Home Watch service is specifically designed for non-rental properties — second homes and seasonal residences that are not rented to vacation travelers. This focus allows us to provide a much more personalized, attentive level of care than a traditional rental management company.

If you have questions about whether your property qualifies, please don't hesitate to reach out.
